Join CoachMate Android Testing

Thank you for your interest in testing our Android app. Please follow these two simple steps.

1 Join Our Tester Group

To get access to the testing program, you need to join our Google Group for testers.

The group is used to manage test access and send important announcements.

Join Tester Group

2 Download the App

After you've joined the group (which is open to all), you can immediately download the test version from Google Play.

Get the App on Google Play
Important: If you see a "Service not available" message on Google Play, it means either:
  • You haven't joined the tester group yet
  • Your request to join hasn't been approved yet
  • You need to wait a few minutes after approval for Google's systems to update
