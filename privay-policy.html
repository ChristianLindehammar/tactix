Privacy Policy for CoachMate

Last updated: January 28, 2024

CoachMate ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Handle

CoachMate is designed to be privacy-focused. We:

  • Do not collect personal information
  • Do not track device information
  • Only store the content you explicitly create (teams, plays, tactics)
  • Keep your data locally on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

Your content (teams, plays, tactics) stays on your device and is only used to:

  • Display your created content within the app
  • Save your work locally

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. All your content remains private and stored locally on your device.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app will remove all associated data.

5. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, your content remains under your control. You can:

  • Delete your content at any time
  • Remove the app to delete all data
