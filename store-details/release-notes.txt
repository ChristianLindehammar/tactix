  What's New in CoachMate 1.3.0
  
  Tactics Board — Cones & Arrows
  Draw up your plays with new tactical tools! Place training cones on the court, and add solid or dashed arrows to illustrate player movements and
  passing lanes. Drag either end of an arrow to adjust it, with gentle snapping to help you draw clean horizontal, vertical, and diagonal lines.

  Bulk Import Players
  Quickly add your entire roster at once — paste a list of player names and they'll be added in one go. No more adding players one by one.

  Smarter Team File Import
  Imported team files are now validated with clear error messages
