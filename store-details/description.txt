Transform your coaching with CoachMate, the all-in-one sports team management and tactics board app. Designed for coaches of floorball, soccer, hockey, bandy, and basketball, CoachMate helps you plan smarter, visualize plays, and manage every detail of your team — anytime, anywhere.

KEY FEATURES

Team Management Made Simple
• Create and organize unlimited teams with custom rosters
• Set starting lineups and manage bench players effortlessly
• Bulk import your entire roster — paste a list of names and add them all at once
• Assign player positions with intuitive drag-and-drop controls
• Import or export team files for sharing and backup

Interactive Court & Field Visualization
• Position players on realistic sport-specific courts and fields
• Use multiple layouts for different scenarios and formations
• Swap players instantly with the visual bench panel
• Adjust formations in real time during planning sessions

Advanced Tactics Board
• Draw and design plays using player and opponent markers
• Place training cones to map out drills and exercises
• Add solid and dashed arrows to illustrate runs, passes, and player movement
• Drag arrow endpoints with angle snapping for clean, precise diagrams
• Add ball or puck markers for full-play visualization
• Ideal for pre-game prep, halftime strategy, or training sessions

Multi-Sport Support
• Soccer/Football – full-field tactics and formations
• Ice Hockey – rink-specific positioning and line setups
• Floorball – indoor court visualization for fast play
• Bandy – large-field tactics and team movement
• Basketball – court layouts for offensive and defensive plays

Privacy-Focused & Offline-First
• All data stored locally on your device
• No account or internet required — start coaching immediately
• Works completely offline, perfect for field- or rink-side use
• Export and share securely when needed
• Available in English, Swedish, and Finnish

Perfect For
• Youth coaches building their first teams
• Professional coaches planning complex strategies
• Assistant coaches managing player rotations
• Sports clubs coordinating multiple teams
• Anyone passionate about tactical sports planning
